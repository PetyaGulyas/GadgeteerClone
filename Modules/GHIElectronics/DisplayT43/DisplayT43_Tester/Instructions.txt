DisplayT43 Tester
---------------
Last Updated: 7/31/2014 by John
Firmware Version: 4.3.3.0

Instructions
------------
1. Connect a DisplayT43 to the RGBT sockets. 
2. Power the mainboard.
3. Touch the screen.

Passing
-------
Near the spot where you touch, three circles will appear. A red
circle will appear in the bottom left of the touch point, a green
circle will appear in the bottom right, and a blue circle will
appear on top. All three form a shape similar to a triangle.

Notes
-----
- You may plug and unplug modules without restarting the mainboard.
