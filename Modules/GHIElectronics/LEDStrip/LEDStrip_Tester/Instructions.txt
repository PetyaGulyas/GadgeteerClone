LEDStrip Tester
---------------
Last Updated: 5/28/2014 by John
SDK Version: 2014 R2 Beta 3
Firmware Version: 4.3.2.0

Instructions
------------
1. Connect an LEDStrip to sockets 1, 9, and 18.
2. Power the mainboard.

Passing
-------
Every led on each LEDStrip will turn on for 1 second when the 
screen says "Relays on" and then turn off for one second when 
the screen says "Relays off" and then repeat.


Notes
-----
- All leds will have the same state, either all on, or all off.
- You may plug and unplug modules without restarting the mainboard.
