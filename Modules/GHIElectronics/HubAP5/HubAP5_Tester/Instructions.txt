HubAP5 Tester
---------------
Last Updated: 7/31/2014 by John
Firmware Version: 4.3.3.0

Instructions
------------
1. Connect a HubAP5 module to socket 2 on the mainboard.
2. Connect a Button module to socket 1 on the hub.
3. Power the mainboard.

Passing
-------
When you press the button, "Button pressed" is displayed 
on the screen and the led on the button is lit. When you
release the button, "Button released" is display on the
screen and the led on the button is not lit.

Notes
-----
- You must restart the mainboard between tests.
