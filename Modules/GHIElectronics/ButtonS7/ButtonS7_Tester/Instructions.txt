ButtonS7 Tester
---------------
Last Updated: 6/2/2014 by John
Firmware Version: 4.3.1.0

Instructions
------------
1. Connect a ButtonS7 to sockets 1.
2. Power the mainboard.

Passing
-------
The screen will display which button is pressed and the
two red LEDs next to the buttons will illuminate when the
button is pressed.

Notes
-----
- You may plug and unplug modules without restarting the mainboard.
