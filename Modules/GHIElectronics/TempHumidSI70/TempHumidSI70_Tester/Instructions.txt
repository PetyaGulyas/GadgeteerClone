TempHumidSI70 Tester
-------------------
Last Updated: 8/7/2014 by John
Firmware Version: 4.3.3.0

Instructions
------------
1. Connect a TempHumidSI70 module to socket 1.
2. Power the mainboard.
3. Surround the module with your hand.

Passing
-------
When you surround the module, the temperature and humidity should increase.

Notes
-----
- You may plug and unplug modules without restarting the mainboard.
- You may use alternative sources of heat and humidity if you wish.
- Room temperature is roughly around 75F or 24C.
- Room humidity will likely be around 40%-60%.
