USBHost Tester
---------------
Last Updated: 6/25/2014 by John
Firmware Version: 4.3.2.0

Instructions
------------
1. Connect a USBHost module to socket 6.
2. Power the mainboard.
3. When the screen says plug in a mouse, plug a mouse
   into the USBHost module.
4. Move the mouse around.
5. Unplug the mouse.

Passing
-------
As you move the mouse, the coordiantes on the screen 
will change.

Notes
-----
- You may plug and unplug modules without restarting the mainboard.
- Do not have a mouse plugged in before powering the mainboard.
