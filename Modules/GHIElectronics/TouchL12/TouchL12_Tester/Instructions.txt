TouchL12 Tester
---------------
Last Updated: 7/31/2014 by John
Firmware Version: 4.3.3.0

Instructions
------------
1. Plug a TouchL12 module into socket 2.
2. Power the mainboard.
3. Move your finger along the strip.

Passing
-------
As you move your finger, the number after "Position" ranges
between 0 and 11. It will be near 0 when you reach the left
end and near 11 when you reach the right end.

Notes
-----
- You must restart the mainboard between tests.
