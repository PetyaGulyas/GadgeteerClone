USBSerialSP Tester
---------------
Last Updated: 9/18/2014 by John
Firmware Version: 4.3.4.0

Instructions
------------
1. Connect a USBSerialSP module to socket 1.
2. Connect a USB cable to the USBSerialSP module and your computer.
3. Open TeraTerm and connect to the serial port that appears in the list.
4. Type into TeraTerm.

Passing
-------
What you type into TeraTerm should appear back in TeraTerm.

Notes
-----
- Do not power the mainboard using the USBClientDP module.
- You must close and reopen TeraTerm between each test.
