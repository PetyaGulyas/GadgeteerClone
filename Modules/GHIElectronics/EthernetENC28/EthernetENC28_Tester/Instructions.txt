EthernetENC28 Tester
---------------
Last Updated: 9/4/2014 by John
Firmware Version: 4.3.3.0

Instructions
------------
1. Connect an EthernetENC28 module to socket 1.
2. Plug an ethernet cable into the ethernet module.
3. Power the mainboard.
4. Press LDR0 when prompted.

Passing
-------
"Module passes" will be displayed in green.

Notes
-----
- You may plug and unplug modules without restarting the mainboard
  when the display says "Press LDR0 to begin"
