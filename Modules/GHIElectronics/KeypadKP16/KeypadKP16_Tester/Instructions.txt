KeypadKP16 Tester
---------------
Last Updated: 6/19/2014 by John
Firmware Version: 4.3.1.0

Instructions
------------
1. Connect a KeypadKP16 to socket 1.
2. Power the mainboard.
3. Press the keys on the module one at a time.

Passing
-------
The screen will display which button is pressed.

Notes
-----
- You may plug and unplug modules without restarting the mainboard.
- Do not press more than one button at once.
