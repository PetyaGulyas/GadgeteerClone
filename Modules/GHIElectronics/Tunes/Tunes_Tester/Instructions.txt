Tunes Tester
---------------
Last Updated: 6/25/2014 by John
Firmware Version: 4.3.2.0

Instructions
------------
1. Connect a Tunes module to socket 18.
2. Power the mainboard.

Passing
-------
The module will emit a tone at 800Hz. It is a moderately high 
frequency noise.


Notes
-----
- The frequency will never change.
- You may plug and unplug modules without restarting the mainboard.
