SDCard Tester
---------------
Last Updated: 8/8/2014 by John
Firmware Version: 4.3.3.0

Instructions
------------
1. Connect an SD card module to socket 9.
2. Power the mainboard.
3. Insert an SD card into the module.

Passing
-------
After inserting a module, "Inserted" and then "Passed" will be
display on the screen.

Notes
-----
- You must restart the mainboard between tests.
